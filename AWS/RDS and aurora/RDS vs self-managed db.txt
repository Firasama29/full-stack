RDS for Postgres vs Self-managed relational databases

- Relational databases are popular choice but require alot of design, infrastructure setting up and management, backup, restore, etc

- RDS provides support for  relational databases: MYSQL, SQL server, POSTGRESQL, MariaDB, Oracle

	- Advantages of RDS:
		helps you focus on your core business application development including:
		- schema design
		- query construction
		- query optimizaation
	
	while the AWS handles the rest of activities such as
		- backup and recovery
		- isolation and security
		- push button scaling
		- automated patching and upgrades
		- advanced monitoring
		- routine monitoring
